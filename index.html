MY NAME IS ELLEN SHEN ROTROFF (row-trough).

I have a business background and have worked in that capacity at companies like Google, Sears, and Cheerily. I learned a lot in those roles, like:

  • how to work on huge cross-functional projects,
  • how to prioritize product needs,
  • how to test and then test some more,
  • how to create an authentic consumer brand,
  • how to work hard but not be too precious with deliverables

Of course, there were other things I did in these roles too: manage inventory, lower margins, and a lot of analyses. At my core though, I always felt most driven when I was working closest to product to create better user experiences. It became clear that if I wanted to focus on that mission, then I needed to change my role - so I made the switch to product design.
